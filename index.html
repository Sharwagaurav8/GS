food menu

Briyani

Biryani is a fragrant and flavorful rice dish cooked with aromatic spices, meat (such as chicken, lamb, or goat), or vegetables, layered together for a harmonious blend of flavors. Originating from the Indian subcontinent, Biryani is renowned for its rich taste and diverse regional variations, ranging from Hyderabadi to Lucknowi styles.

price: ₹2500

Dal Tadka

A nutritious lentil stew tempered with spices, rich in protein and essential nutrients.Dal Tadka is a traditional Indian dish consisting of yellow lentils cooked to perfection and tempered with a tantalizing mix of spices like cumin, mustard seeds, garlic, and dried red chilies.

price: ₹500

Idli Sambar

Idli Sambar is a popular South Indian delicacy comprising soft, fluffy rice cakes (idlis) served with a flavorful and aromatic lentil-based soup known as sambar. The idlis, made from fermented rice and lentil batter, offer a light and nutritious base, while the sambar, infused with a medley of vegetables and spices, provides a rich source of protein, fiber, and essential vitamins.

price: ₹1500

Palak Paneer

Palak Paneer is a classic North Indian dish featuring creamy spinach curry combined with chunks of paneer (Indian cottage cheese), creating a satisfying vegetarian meal. Bursting with flavors from aromatic spices like cumin, coriander, and garam masala, this dish is not only delicious but also rich in iron, calcium, and vitamins.

price: ₹1000

Sabudana Khichdi

Sabudana Khichdi is a popular fasting dish in India made from tapioca pearls sautéed with peanuts, potatoes, and spices. This gluten-free and energy-boosting meal is often consumed during religious fasts due to its high carbohydrate content, providing a quick source of energy.

price: ₹2000

Raita

Raita is a refreshing yogurt-based condiment mixed with chopped vegetables like cucumber, tomatoes, or onions, and seasoned with spices like cumin and mint. Known for its cooling effect, Raita complements spicy Indian dishes, aids digestion, and provides a source of probiotics from yogurt.

price: ₹100
